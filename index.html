About

Artificial intelligence is no longer regulated at the margins. Legal and policy developments now shape core technical decisions, from model training and dataset construction to evaluation, deployment, and access controls.

This monthly newsletter provides concise updates on local and global AI legislation, case law, and policy measures that may affect research agendas, grant compliance, open-source practices, and downstream deployment.
